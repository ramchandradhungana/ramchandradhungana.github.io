Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  ramchandradhungana.github.io

This website is a personal academic portfolio for Ram Chandra Dhungana. It does not sell products, run advertisements, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. This policy explains what limited data is collected and why.

1. Information We Collect

This site collects only what you voluntarily provide through the contact form:

  • Name — so I can address you by name in my response.
  • Email address — so I can reply to your inquiry.
  • Subject / Inquiry Type — to understand the nature of your request.
  • Message content — the body of your inquiry.

No account registration, payment information, or sensitive personal data is collected on this site.

2. How Your Information Is Used

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry (research collaboration, consulting, tutoring, etc.).
  • Follow up on ongoing academic or professional conversations.

Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

This site uses two external services that may process limited data:

Formspree (formspree.io) — handles contact form submissions. When you submit the form, your name, email, and message are transmitted to Formspree's servers and then forwarded to my email inbox. Formspree operates under its own Privacy Policy.

Tidio (tidio.com) — provides the live chat widget visible in the bottom corner of the site. Tidio may use cookies to maintain chat session state. No personal data you share in chat is used for advertising. Tidio operates under its own Privacy Policy.

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages (github.com). GitHub may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) as part of hosting. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

4. Cookies

This site does not set any first-party cookies. The Tidio chatbot widget may set session-level cookies required for chat functionality. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to address your inquiry. Email threads are retained in accordance with standard professional email practices. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting me directly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (including the EU/EEA under GDPR), you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data I hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact me directly at r0dhun01@louisville.edu.

7. Children's Privacy

This site is intended for academic and professional audiences. It does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, please contact me so I can delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Material changes will be noted by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
