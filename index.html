About

Academic Profile

I am a Biostatistics PhD candidate and research assistant at the University of Louisville.

My academic background spans biostatistics, statistics, computational mathematics, and pure mathematics. I work with clinical and healthcare data using statistical modeling, survival analysis, regression modeling, study design, and statistical programming in SAS, R, Python, and SQL.

I am especially interested in clinical trials, oncology research, biomarker-informed designs, reproducible analysis, and clear interpretation of applied biomedical findings.
